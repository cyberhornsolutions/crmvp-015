Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is our trading platform?
A: Our trading platform is an online system that allows you to buy and sell various financial instruments such as stocks, bonds, and cryptocurrencies.
Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and follow the registration process.

Account Management

Q: How can I reset my password?
A: You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your registered email address.
Q: Can I have multiple trading accounts?
A: Yes, you can have multiple trading accounts with us. Contact our support team for assistance with setting up additional accounts.

Trading and Investments

Q: How can I place a trade?
A: To place a trade, log in to your account, select the financial instrument you want to trade, specify the quantity and other details, and click "Place Order."
Q: What are the trading hours?
A: Our trading platform operates 24/7, but specific trading hours for different assets may vary. Check the asset's trading hours in the platform.
